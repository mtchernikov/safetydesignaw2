The system is a passenger bridge mounted on a vertical reciprocating conveyor.
The bridge platform moves vertically to align with a vehicle, aircraft, ship or train entrance.
Passengers and staff can stand on the bridge platform during boarding and may be near the platform edge.
The platform is moved by an electric motor and lift mechanism. A motor controller controls the movement.
The system uses a 230 V AC electrical supply for the motor controller.
Guide rails are located close to the moving platform.
A landing gate is intended to prevent access to the moving zone and platform edge.
A presence sensor detects people or obstructions in the movement area.
An emergency stop is provided.
The installation may be exposed to rain, wind and moisture.
Some covers and cable trays are made of plastic.
The design description does not yet define safety-rated interlocks, stopping distance, load holding, overload detection, emergency lowering, insulation concept, or protection against moisture ingress.
